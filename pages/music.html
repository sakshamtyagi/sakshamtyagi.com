My Tracks

Jazbaat

"Jazbaat" isn't just a song—it's the heartbeat of my artistic journey. It marks the very first step I took as both a creator and an independent artist. This track holds an incredibly special place in my heart because it’s where everything began. It was my very first release, the spark that ignited the fire within me to pursue music not just as a passion, but as a purpose. When I released Jazbaat on 9 May 2021, I had no idea the impact it would have—not just on my career, but on the way I connect with people through sound and storytelling. This single track inspired me to launch my YouTube channel dedicated to music distribution, which later became a platform not only for my own creativity but also for guiding and empowering other independent artists navigating their way through the music industry. Jazbaat taught me the ropes. Through its release, I learned how to distribute music, build my brand, engage with a community, and turn raw emotion into something that could touch others. It became the foundation for all the content I’ve created, the relationships I’ve built, and the mission I now stand for—to educate, inspire, and uplift fellow indie musicians. Re-releasing this song on 22 March 2025 feels like a full-circle moment. It’s more than just a remaster or a revival—it’s a tribute to the roots of my journey, a celebration of how far we’ve come, and a promise that I’ll keep growing, learning, and creating with even more heart.

Khabar

"Khabar" is more than just a collaboration - it's a powerful union of two artistic souls and a testament to what passion, precision, and purpose can create together. This track holds a unique place in my journey, not only as a co-artist but as the producer who brought every element to life with full intention and deep homework. Every beat, every word, and every emotion in Khabar has been carefully crafted and poured into with dedication. Unlike my solo beginning with Jazbaat, this time I had the privilege of sharing the creative space with another incredibly talented artist—making this release a shared heartbeat, a conversation in melody. Producing Khabar challenged and shaped me in new ways. From conceptualization to final mastering, I was immersed in every layer—ensuring the sound resonated as deeply as the message behind it. This song was built with vision, discipline, and a whole lot of heart. It's a reminder that growth often means stepping into new roles, collaborating fearlessly, and learning from one another. Khabar doesn’t just represent a song—it’s proof that when artists come together with a shared purpose, something truly meaningful can emerge.
