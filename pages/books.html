My Books

The Distribution Blueprint - EBook


The eBook edition of The Distribution Blueprint is the ideal companion for emerging artists and music creators who want to understand - and own - the process of distributing their work in today’s digital-first landscape. This is my first book, and I’ve poured everything I’ve learned into it: from grassroots experiences to professional insight, all captured in a compact, easy-to-navigate digital format. Whether you’re taking your first step or scaling up as an independent artist, this guide is tailored for fast, accessible learning on-the-go. This edition is structured like a self-paced masterclass, offering hyperlinked chapters, interactive checklists, and practical takeaways designed to help you implement each step immediately. You’ll move from the basics of music distribution to complex topics like revenue sharing, distributor switching, and legal licensing. Each chapter builds on the last, giving you a clear roadmap to take control of your releases - efficiently and professionally. Perfect for artists who learn by doing and want a quick, smart tool in their pocket.

The Distribution Blueprint - Paper Back


The paperback edition of The Distribution Blueprint is more than just a printed version - it’s a physical manifestation of your creative ambition. Crafted for readers who learn best with a pen in hand, this edition gives you space to annotate, highlight, and shape the book into a personalized action guide. It’s a foundational text for your studio desk, music journal, or shelf of essentials—ready to help whether you’re uploading your debut single or managing a label’s catalog. What sets this edition apart is the depth and structure it brings to your learning experience. Divided into 33 detailed chapters, the book walks you through the full spectrum of music distribution - from “What is Music Distribution?” to navigating official artist channels, enabling caller tunes, legally releasing cover songs, and resolving copyright claims. Whether it’s understanding UPC & ISRC codes, handling collaborators, or exploring monetization strategies like AdSense vs Content ID, the paperback gives you the full professional playbook - laid out clearly with real-world advice and examples. This is my first book, written to cover everything you need - starting from the absolute basics and leading all the way into advanced strategies for long-term success. It’s more than a guide - it’s your blueprint to independence in the music world.
